The Doc is a Companion who lives in the Slums, member of the Outsiders. In his Secret Laboratory, he was trying to create a weapon against Zurks (the Defluxor). One day he left to make a ‚Äúreal-life test‚Äù of the weapon, and never came back. He could not return because of a blown Fuse that started the Generator powering the Defluxor. It started up loudly, attracting Zurks to it, so the Doc couldn't replace it himself, and was stuck in a Dead End for many years. After the Cat helps him, Doc installs a small Defluxor on B-12 and returns to the Slums. Doc is missed by his son, Seamus, who refers to him as "Papa" and "Dad". The game doesn't say if Doc is a single father or if he has a partner. Seamus has spent many years waiting for news of his father, and he helps track him down with a Tracker.

Momo says of Doc, "He was really a huge nerd for electronics and stuff. He was always on the verge of a great discovery!"

He had a notebook, for information about it, called Doc's Notebook.

Trivia

  • Both Doc and Seamus are based (visually and behaviorally) on Dr. Emmett Brown and Marty McFly from the Back to the Future movie series.
  • The code to Doc‚Äôs lab is hidden in a time-related puzzle.
  • Doc's incorrect spelling of "gigaWatt" is likely a reference to the mockery the original movie received due to incorrectly pronouncing the word as "JiggaWatts" with a soft G. Surprisingly, the movie got the amount right, though: a lightning bolt can easily provide 1.21 GW, or even about a thousand times that (~10 TW).
  • In the game's source code, Doc is often referred to as ROBERTO: DROID_DE_MeetRoberto etc. It was likely an earlier name that was changed in the course of development. This does not seem to be a name from the Credits. Another characters that seems to have been renamed are Zbaltazar (Gerard), Seamus (Roberto Jr), Kosma (Koba), Riko (Freddy) and Bilite (Billie).
  • He has 5 tattoos/stickers on his body: " 7G' ", a beaker, an atom, red and yellow stickers with unknown symbols.
