Clementine is one of the Companions found in the game Stray. As an Outsider, she is one of the major characters in the game. Momo said about her, "She was really brave you know, the most fearless person I have ever met."

Clementine used to live in her apartment in the Slums until she decided to find a way to the Outside with Zbaltazar . Somehow having made their way through the Sewers, they stopped for a while in an Antvillage. Clementine taught the kids there and then went to Midtown, but Zbaltazar stayed in the Antvillage without her.

In Midtown, she studied Subway work, old machines, and the Neco Corporation. She stayed in a cozy apartment in the Residence. At some point, he was put on the wanted list as a rebel and an Outsider, and she remained hiding in her apartment. When the cat came to her apartment, she told him about her plan and gave him a message to find Blazer - they were not well acquainted, but she knew that he could help her with the Atomic Battery that is needed to power the Subway.

While the cat goes to the factory, Clementine leaves him a note and meets Blazer in a Nightclub, but it turns out to be a trap, and Blazer turns her over to the Sentinels, ties her up and waits for the cat in the VIP room of the club. After the cat arrives there, he and Clementine get sent to Jail. When they meet there, Clementine asks him to help bring her the key and escapes her prison cell. After that, they go to look for a way out of Jail, but on the way the cat helps B-12 get out, and they go on together, trying not to get caught by the Sentinels.

After the cat opens the last door, Clementine drives out of the Jail in a stolen truck into the Midtown while she is being chased by the Sentinels. Hiding the cat at the Subway Station, she gives him the keys to the train and leaves to distract the Sentinels. When the cat reaches the Control Room and opens the roof of the city, Clementine is almost caught by the Sentinels, but they get disconnected due to the shutdown of the secutity protocol and fall to the ground.

Trivia

  • Clementine loves poetry. Alexander and Bonobot were her friends.
  • She has 6 hats in her Midtown apartment, including the one she is wearing and one used as a flower pot.
  • She has 10 tattoos: "OH", "011", "Silent", Outsiders logo, "KISS ME", half black-half white heart and 4 unreadable text tattoos.
  • Most Companions have a few dead pixels around their faces. Clementine, however, has an entire chunk missing from the side. This appears on every expression except the computer expression, which is somehow displayed on those dead pixels. Her eyes and mouth colors are red, blue and green.
