Background

Initially encountered as a disembodied voice within the flat, the Cat aids it in acquiring a body, B-12 is the consciousness of a human scientist that attempted to upload his consciousness to the city's network. This upload was partially successful; however, the scientist's memories of who he was became corrupted as the process did not complete successfully, with his only memory being that he has to go outside and he was with a Scientist before humans departed from the city. As means towards leaving the city, B-12 allies with the Cat, providing him a harness and means of lighting paths, interacting with robots, items and complex technologies such as keypads and the Defluxor.

As the Cat and B-12 journey to the outside, B-12 will gradually recover his missing Memories.

Trivia

  • B-12's name is a reference to the developers, BlueTwelve Studio.
  • B-12's box calls him a "Drone Companion" - so Drones may be a subtype of Companion, rather than a separate kind of robot. Alternatively, the WT 36K-129 model may simply be a drone created to serve as (the body of) a Companion.
  • The name B-12 comes from the company "B-12 Robotics" that manufactured the drone (see the box texture shown to the right).
  • There are droid letters on B-12's body, which means that some Companion used the drone before.

Dialog

B-12 is the character with the most dialog in Stray, but it mostly falls into a few categories. For brevity and relevance, it is transcribed in those places, rather than here.



  • Translating Companions' speech - this is considered dialogue for the individual companions, and is listed on their pages.
  • Translating writing - B-12 can occasionally be asked to read or translate some writing in-game.
  • Memories - Some of these are triggered simply by entering an area where B-12 sees something, others require interaction to activate.
  • Examining inventory items - B-12 will provide help and advice on almost any inventory item you can find in the game.
  • Help on quest status - B-12 can be asked for advice on quest progress at almost any time.
  • B-12 Dialog - The remainder of B-12's dialog has its own page, because there's still a lot of it.
