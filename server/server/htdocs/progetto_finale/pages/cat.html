Characters are found in almost all the Chapters throughout Stray. Protagonist and player character in every chapter is the red "stray" Cat. Eventually, in Chapter 3: The Flat, Cat is joined by B-12, a small flying drone, which quickly becomes a great companion for Cat.

The city inhabitants turn out to be robotic "Companions", who act like their human predecessors.

Throughout Stray

The ginger was born outside the Walled City. He took shelter with his family Inside the Wall of the city, where he could sleep, hunt and play. One day while exploring the territory, the cat slipped while jumping onto a broken pipe, triggering the events of the game. Having fallen, the only path he could take was into the depths of the mysterious cybercity, in hopes of finding a way out.

In the game, he is assisted by a drone that prompts the cat and communicates with the residents of the City by translating their language. The entire game is seen from a cats perspective - this allows for unique movement mechanics, and allows for cat-like interactions with objects and characters, like knocking over a paint tin, scratching a door to enter, or simply taking a nap.

Cat Family

The cat has a family or group (officially, in the description of the game, it is called a family). These are the three other cats at the start of the game. Their colors are orange with white spots, white with orange spots and black. The cat's family is only seen in Chapter 1: Inside the Wall.

Appereance

He appears to be a ginger tabby cat with green eyes. He has darker orange stripes, modeled after Murtaugh. Later in the game, the cat is seen wearing a backpack, which is black with a white trim, or multi-colored if every Memory is collected in-game. Badges can also be collected and will show on the side of the backpack. The cats backpack has a compartment for his drone friend B-12. .
