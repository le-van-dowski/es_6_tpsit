plot


The game starts off with the player prompted to interact with their family, and once they have all been interacted with, the player falls asleep with them on a flattened cardboard box. When the player does this, a cutscene plays the next in-game day as a butterfly lands on their ear, awakening from their long slumber and following their friends down the pipes and platforms scattered across the area until they reach the ground. This is also where the name of this chapter is first revealed.

Once the cutscene has ended, the cat is prompted to do things like crawl under tight spaces, scratch on trees and drink from puddles of water. The player is taught the basic mechanics of Stray and learns that, by meowing, they can relocate the other cats and get back on track. The other cats also seem to do this when they get stuck behind.

After a while of walking with the other cats, the player finds themselves with a loose pipe. Their cat friends who go before the player seem to easily manage this gap, but the pipe is loosening after every jump. The player attempts to cross, but slips and holds on for dear life as their friends watch in confusion, eventually leaving the player exhausted and causing them to fall down.

The player falls down into a barren sewer system, injuring their leg and limping for a small period of time before falling tired. Suddenly, as if magic, a large door opens and a bright red light flashes from it, enough for the player to enter, and before them, two Zurks who find themselves rummaging in a few trash bags.
